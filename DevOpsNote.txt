What is DevOps?
Bings the development team and the operation team together 

DevOps practices:
CI/CD - Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivry, Continuous Deployment


Why DevOPs?

- Communication between the Development team and Operation team (no isolated team)
- Faster development of product 
- Adaptable to changes 
- Reduce cost and increase profits 


4 pillars of DevOps

- Ease of use
- Flexibility
- Robustness
- Cost


Risks

Dev Environment broken - Low/Medium
Testing server broken - Low/Medium
Automated testing broken - Low/Medium
Jenkins server broken - Low/Medium
Production server fails - High
